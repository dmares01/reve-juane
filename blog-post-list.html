Como Park

May 8, 2020 by Drew Mares

Ahh Como Park, in a weird way its where all of this began. I don't have any pictures from the original walk that happened in August from sophomore year, but you look cute in this one so I used it anyways. We've gone to Como plenty of times, but none have come close to being as crazy as that first time. Staying up all night long (I don't even know if I could do an all nighter anymore) and then walking to Como when the sun came up.
